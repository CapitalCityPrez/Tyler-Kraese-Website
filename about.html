About Us

At Capital City Accounting, we bring over 10 years of experience in accounting and bookkeeping services, internal and external audits, and financial strategy. Our team is dedicated to helping businesses achieve financial clarity and growth through tailored solutions.

Our founder, Tyler Kraese, is a seasoned Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and Certified Information System Auditor (CISA) with expertise in invoice processing, financial reconciliations, budgeting, financial reporting, etc. We pride ourselves on delivering accurate, reliable, and strategic financial services to businesses of all sizes.
